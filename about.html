Sebastien Payannet

Sebastien is an award winning designer based in Los Angeles. Currently a senior designer at Oculus Light Studio, he has worked as the lead designer on a wide breadth of projects internationally and domestically ranging from high-end hospitality and vast mixed-use developments, to retails and residences.

His projects have been featured in publications such as Interiors Magazine, Architectural Digest, Forbes, Restaurant Hospitality, and more.


Forming a foundation to his creative work is an Architectural Engineering degree from the University of Colorado, Boulder.


Outside of design, Sebastien is also an accomplished figure skater, notably competing at the 2018 & 2022 US Olympic Trials and six US National Championships.
